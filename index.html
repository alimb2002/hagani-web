About Us


Biography
Shahrokh Haghani

I am a mortgage broker and finance professional with over 15 years of experience. I can speak English and Persian both fluently. With access to over 30 of Australia's leading lenders, I can compare hundreds of loans for you and provide you with the most appropriate option that suits your needs. My specialties include home loans for: First Home Buyers, Owner Occupied, Investment Loans, Refinancing, Construction, Commercial Lending, Debt Consolidation and Self-employed borrowers, and Car Loans. I am fully accredited by the Finance Brokers Association of Australia (FBAA) and hold a Diploma in Financial Services (Finance/Mortgage Broking) - so you know you're in safe hands.
